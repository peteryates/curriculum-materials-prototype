Lesson overview

Core knowledge for all pupils

  • he used the Court of Star Chamber to fine his enemies.
  • he confiscated land from some nobles.
  • when he made treaties with foreign countries, he often demanded payment from those countries.
  • he forced wealthy people to give him loans and gifts.
  • Henry employed two clever officials – Sir Richard Empson and Sir Edmund Dudley – to do all this.

Vocabulary for this learning objective

  • coffer
  • treaty
  • sovereign
  • confiscate
  • appoint
  • judicial

Building on previous knowledge

Vocabulary

Pupils should already know:

  • Tudor: relating to the English royal dynasty which held the throne from the accession of Henry VII in 1485 until the death of Elizabeth I in 1603.
  • noble: belonging by rank, title, or birth to the aristocracy.
  • accession: the attainment or acquisition of a position of rank or power.
